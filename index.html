About Aero Mix Martial Art

Aero Mix Martial Art is a form of martial art based on the fundamentals of foot works, kicks, throws, punches, blocks, locks, chokes etc. It is practiced and mastered on music. Like all other martial arts Aero Mix Martial Art is progressive and has a belt gradation system. 

For the masses, it is a form of free style martial arts practiced on music, to enable them to achieve fitness levels and the music keeps them engaged and motivated. This also aids them in learning self defense techniques. We have done self defense workshops based on the skills learnt in Aero Mix Martial Arts for several educational institutions i.e. schools and colleges, corporates and also for the NCC camp for the Army Annual Training Camp in Mumbai. 

For the Martial Arts Professionals and practitioners, people practicing Aero Mix Martial Art can participate in tournaments for other combat sports such as Taekwondo, Boxing, Wushu, Judo and Karate. This is because the basic skills used in these martial art forms are included in Aero Mix Martial Art. For people pursuing this as a martial art form and practice regularly, will benefit from increased speed, agility, strength, endurance, enhanced cognitive skills, reaction time. It involves an holistic development of the learner and makes them more confident and aware about their environment.
